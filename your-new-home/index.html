    The Perfect Location

    Close to many amenities relevant to seniors including  the Vista Farmers’ Market, religious services, doctor and dental.

    •  Physician’s services – There are a number of medical offices covering a range of specialties less than one mile from the property.  Some of those include Graybill Medical Group (physicians), Life Quality USA (rehab and physical therapy) and Psychological Health Center.
    • Medical Offices – Within 4 miles is Tri-City Medical Center and its extensive surrounding complex of every imaginable medical specialty.
    • Community Activity Sites – Nearby community activity sites include
      • Breeze Hill Park
      • Vista Village Creek
      • Vista Library
      • Vista Farmer’s Market
      • Civic Center Park
      • Brengle Terrace
      • Gloria McClellan Senior Center is within 4 miles. There are several churches along S. Melrose that offer organized activities. Additionally, many schools and churches bring activities to our home.
