There are several churches along S. Melrose that offer organized activities. <\/li><\/ol><\/li>

          • Dressing;<\/li>
          • Eating (including feeding assistance);<\/li>
          • Toileting; <\/li>
          • Bathing;<\/li>
          • Grooming;<\/li>
          • Mobility; and<\/li>

          • Clean bed and bath linens weekly, or as often as needed. <\/li>
          • Cleaning of resident\u2019s room at least weekly.<\/li>
          • Comfortable and suitable bed and bedroom furniture. The resident may provide his\/her own furniture as long as the room will accommodate items. Furniture should be of good quality and safe. Facility reserves the right to reject furnishings if facility believes the furniture is not safe or in a state of good repair. <\/li>
          • Assistance in meeting necessary medical and dental needs as follows: Facility encourages responsible person to transport resident to medical and dental appointments. Facility will transport resident as needed to medical and dental appointments, if responsible person is unavailable. <\/li>
